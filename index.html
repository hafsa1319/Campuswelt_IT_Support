IT Support 


This course is for training the support personnel needed by the IT industry. has been designed to help delegates understand how to become an IT specialist and learn the vital techniques, some basic procedures, tools, applications, and duties of an IT Service Desk Analyst to fulfill this role.
In this course, you learn a lot of tips & tricks to improve Service Desk performance. You learn Incident and Request classification, and specific features of performing major Incident resolving. You learn and practice on creating Incident and Request models for certain types of user calls. While Monitoring/Event management studies You learn the best practices of building-in monitoring tools in an IT service, types of events, and their management of them.

What You Will Learn:

If you have a career goal in the IT sector, you will gain the practical skills you need in business life and enter the IT career life with this course with expert and certified trainers.


  • Learn how to provide end-to-end customer support, ranging from identifying problems to troubleshooting and debugging,
  • Troubleshooting basic IT issues
  • Solve IT Tickets / work on continuous improvement of all IT-Processes
  • How a Support / Service Desk operates
  • Gain skills required to succeed in an entry-level IT job
  • Operating Systems: Windows 10, Active Directory, Office 365, and other IT support skills
